FAQ

What is the purpose of this website?

The goal of this tiny website is to give everyone easy access to all the stuff that happens around the LEC on Twitter.

How does it work?

Basically this website is just a tiny wrapper around the awesome list feature that Twitter provides.

I have discovered a bug or have a suggestion for a feature. Where can I submit this feedback?

Please create an issue at github or reach out to me on Twitter.

Which accounts are displayed here?

The entire list can be seen at Twitter.

Why is player/coach/whoever xyz missing in the list?

In order to get the list of all relevant people I looked at the current rosters on liquipedia. However, some people seem to not have a Twitter account (or I wasn't able to find it). Also, some people don't write their tweets in english and are thus also not on this list.

Why are there only 20 tweets here?

Unluckily, Twitter does not support more than 20 tweets for this list view. If people like and use lecbanter.gg, the number of tweets could be increased in order to show an infinite scrolling list.

How can I contribute to this project?

The best way to contribute is submitting a pull request on github.
